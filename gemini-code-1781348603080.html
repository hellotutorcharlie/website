The Instructor Profile

Hi there! My name is Charlie. I am a tutor passionate about helping kids master English and Chinese through methods that are naturally engaging, active, and fun.

I was born and raised in Los Angeles, California, and have spent the last 12 years living and studying in Taiwan. This cross-cultural background gives me a native command of both conversational landscapes, allowing me to naturally bridge language barriers for youth learners.

My approach is modeled around dynamic peer guidance—like an older brother leading an interactive learning adventure. Children thrive when lessons align with their interests, which is why I replace boring structural drills with logic gameplay, music production, and hands-on crafting environments.
